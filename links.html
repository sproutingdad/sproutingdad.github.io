Links

This is a list of links to other websites and resources that I find interesting or useful.

Chromatone - A tool developed by one of my favorite tattoo artists that encodes color images into monochrome patterns. I see many potential useful applications for this tool, such as fiber arts, printmaking, and graphic design.

Cobalt - A tool for saving video, audio, photos, or gifs from most websites.

Sitescroll - An index of personal websites. A great resource for designing my own site.

Mindy Seu are.na - Curated collections of interesting design, research, and technology by Mindy Seu.

Falling Fruit - A crowdsourced map of edible plants in your area.
