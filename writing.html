Ten Today

Some of my writing can be found on Ten Today.

Ten Today is a collaborative media project founded by me and six co-creators. We seek to post experimental forms of writing and media. The site explores both new and familiar formats through continuously evolving work that reimagines and subverts the hyper-digestible style of listicles. Our objective is to co-opt search engine optimization’s restructuring of media and attention to give readers something worth mulling over.

All posts on the site are anonymous. This allows contributors to explore ideas freely without the constraints of public attribution. Anyone can post on Ten Today; reach out if you have an idea.

In addition to writing, we host events around New York City to build a community around the site.
